# Business Model

## Sustainable Revenue & Path to Profitability

### Revenue Streams
- **Product Sales**: 20-25% blended gross margin across all product categories
- **Delivery Fees**: 500-750 XAF per order (free above 10,000 XAF)
- **Subscription Model**: "Okada Prime" at 2,500-3,000 XAF monthly (Year 2)
- **Future Revenue**: B2B services, advertising, and data insights (Year 3+)

### Unit Economics (Per Order)
| Metric | Amount (XAF) | % |
|--------|--------------|---|
| Average Order Value | 8,500 | 100% |
| Net Revenue (after VAT) | 7,189 | 84.6% |
| Variable Costs | (6,248) | 73.5% |
| **Contribution Margin** | **941** | **11.1%** |

### Path to Profitability
- **Year 1**: Negative EBITDA (-46.5M XAF) as we invest in infrastructure
- **Year 2**: Positive EBITDA (124.6M XAF) through operational efficiency
- **Year 3**: Strong profitability (562.9M XAF) with 63.5% EBITDA margin

### Operational Footprint
- **Year 1**: 4 dark stores in Douala & Yaoundé
- **Year 2**: 8 dark stores in Douala & Yaoundé
- **Year 3**: 12 dark stores across 3 cities

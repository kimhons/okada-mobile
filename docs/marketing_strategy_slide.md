# Marketing Strategy

## Building a Beloved Local Brand

### Brand Positioning
"Cameroon's trusted, everyday convenience partner"

**Core Message**: "Okada: Your Market, Delivered in Minutes"

### Customer Acquisition Strategy
- **Pre-Launch**: Teaser campaign, waitlist building, early adopter incentives
- **Launch**: Influencer partnerships, street team activations, referral program
- **Growth**: Content marketing, local business partnerships, performance marketing

### Multi-Channel Marketing Approach
- **Digital**: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, YouTube
- **Offline**: Street marketing, branded riders, community events, radio

### Community Engagement
- **Okada Rider Program**: Positioning riders as trusted brand ambassadors
- **Local Supplier Program**: "Made in Cameroon" initiative highlighting local sourcing
- **Neighborhood Engagement**: Sponsoring local events and community initiatives

### Key Performance Indicators
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): < 2,500 XAF
- First-Order Conversion Rate: 25%
- Customer Retention Rate (CRR): 35%
- Lifetime Value (LTV): > 7,500 XAF

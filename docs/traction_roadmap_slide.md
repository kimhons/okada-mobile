# Traction & Roadmap

## Execution Plan & Growth Strategy

### Launch Timeline
- **Q1 2026**: Pre-launch activities, team building, and technology development
- **Q2 2026**: Pilot launch in select neighborhoods of Douala
- **Q3 2026**: Full launch in Douala and expansion to first neighborhoods in Yaoundé
- **Q4 2026**: Complete coverage of major neighborhoods in Douala and Yaoundé

### Growth Projections
| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Dark Stores | 4 | 8 | 12 |
| Daily Orders | 200 | 800 | 1,800 |
| Annual Orders | 73,000 | 292,000 | 657,000 |
| Active Customers | 15,000 | 45,000 | 90,000 |

### Expansion Strategy
- **Phase 1**: Establish strong presence in Douala and Yaoundé
- **Phase 2**: Expand to third major city (Bafoussam or Bamenda)
- **Phase 3**: Introduce new verticals (pharmacy, electronics, etc.)
- **Phase 4**: Regional expansion to neighboring countries

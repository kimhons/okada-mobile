## 4. Multi-Channel Marketing Campaign Framework

Okada will employ an integrated multi-channel marketing strategy that combines the broad reach of digital platforms with the high-touch impact of offline engagement. The framework is designed to build brand awareness, drive customer acquisition, and foster a strong sense of community around the brand.

### 4.1. Digital Marketing Channels

Digital marketing will be the cornerstone of Okada's marketing efforts, leveraging the high mobile and social media penetration in Cameroon's urban centers.

| Channel | Objective | Key Tactics | KPIs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Social Media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)** | Brand awareness, community engagement, customer acquisition | - Daily content creation (French, English, Pidgin)<br>- Short-form video (Reels, TikToks)<br>- Geo-targeted ad campaigns<br>- Influencer collaborations<br>- Social commerce (Facebook/Instagram Shops) | Reach, engagement rate, follower growth, cost per acquisition (CPA), conversion rate |
| **WhatsApp Marketing** | Customer communication, direct sales, retention | - Welcome messages for new users<br>- Order confirmations and delivery updates<br>- Broadcast lists for promotions and new product announcements<br>- Customer support and feedback collection | Open rate, click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, customer satisfaction (CSAT) |
| **Content Marketing (Blog, YouTube)** | Brand building, SEO, customer education | - Blog posts on local food, recipes, and urban life<br>- How-to videos and tutorials (e.g., "5-minute meals with Okada")<br>- Supplier and rider spotlight stories | Website traffic, time on page, video views, search engine rankings |
| **Influencer Marketing** | Trust building, reach, user-generated content (UGC) | - Partner with local food bloggers, lifestyle influencers, and comedians<br>- Focus on micro-influencers (10k-50k followers) for authentic engagement<br>- Launch day "takeovers" and unboxing videos | Reach, engagement rate, UGC volume, influencer-driven conversions |
| **Performance Marketing (SEM, ASO)** | Drive high-intent traffic and app downloads | - Google Ads campaigns targeting relevant keywords<br>- App Store Optimization (ASO) for Google Play Store<br>- Retargeting campaigns for website visitors and cart abandoners | Cost per click (CPC), click-through rate (CTR), app store ranking, download velocity |

### 4.2. Offline Marketing Channels

Offline marketing will be used to build trust and reach customers in a tangible, memorable way, particularly in the initial launch phase.

| Channel | Objective | Key Tactics | KPIs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Street Marketing & Activations** | Hyper-local brand awareness, on-the-spot acquisitions | - Branded kiosks in high-traffic areas (markets, transport hubs)<br>- Street teams with tablets for instant app downloads<br>- Flyer distribution in residential and commercial areas | Number of flyers distributed, number of on-the-spot downloads, CPA |
| **Okada Rider Network** | Mobile advertising, brand visibility | - Branded jackets, helmets, and delivery boxes for all riders<br>- Riders act as brand ambassadors, distributing referral codes | Brand recall, rider-driven referrals, word-of-mouth mentions |
| **Community Events & Sponsorships** | Community engagement, brand building | - Sponsor local football matches, cultural festivals, and neighborhood events<br>- Host Okada-branded cooking competitions or food tastings | Brand mentions, event attendance, lead generation |
| **Radio Advertising** | Mass-market brand awareness | - Short, catchy jingles on popular local radio stations<br>- Focus on drive-time slots to reach commuters | Reach, frequency, ad recall, website traffic spikes |

### 4.3. Phased Campaign Rollout

The marketing campaigns will be rolled out in three distinct phases, each with a specific objective and channel mix.

- **Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Awareness & Anticipation):** Focus on digital channels (social media, landing page) to build a waitlist and generate buzz.
- **Phase 2: Launch (Acquisition & Trial):** A full-funnel approach combining a digital blitz with hyper-local offline activations to drive initial downloads and orders.
- **Phase 3: Growth (Engagement & Loyalty):** Shift focus to content marketing, community building, and retention-focused campaigns to build a loyal customer base.

This integrated, multi-channel approach will enable Okada to build a strong brand presence in Cameroon, effectively reach its target audience, and drive sustainable growth.

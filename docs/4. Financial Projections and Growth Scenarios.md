### 4.3. Growth Scenarios

**Conservative Scenario:**

- Slower adoption of quick commerce, resulting in lower order volumes (25-30% below projection).
- Higher customer acquisition costs due to a more competitive market.
- Slower path to profitability, with breakeven potentially delayed to Year 3 or 4.

**Aggressive Scenario:**

- Rapid adoption of quick commerce, leading to higher order volumes (40-50% above projection).
- Faster expansion to new cities and a larger dark store network.
- Higher initial investment required, but a faster path to significant market share and profitability.

### 4.4. Funding Requirements

Based on the financial projections, Okada will require an initial seed investment to cover the startup costs and the operational losses in the first year. A seed round of **$250,000 - $350,000 USD (approx. 150-210 million XAF)** would be sufficient to fund the first 12-18 months of operations and achieve a positive EBITDA run rate.

Subsequent funding rounds (Series A) will be required to finance the expansion in Year 2 and beyond, with the amount depending on the growth trajectory and market conditions.

These financial projections demonstrate a clear path to profitability for Okada, driven by a scalable business model and strong unit economics. The key to success will be disciplined execution, continuous optimization of operations, and a deep understanding of the local market dynamics of the Cameroonian market.
